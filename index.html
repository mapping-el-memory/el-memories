Welcome to Mapping East Lansing Memories!

Mapping East Lansing Memories is an interpersonal archive, functioning as a case study of the affective impact of space and place on initializing one's associative lived experience.

Hover over the markers on the map (see images below) to make the options appear. To learn about the different places per team member, toggle through each of the markers. These markers represent different locaations across East Lansing, chronologically arranged as our team members visited them.
